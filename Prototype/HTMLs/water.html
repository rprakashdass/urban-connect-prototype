Water Projects

Water Supply System Upgradation

A comprehensive project aimed at upgrading the water supply system, improving water quality and increasing access to clean water for all citizens.

  • Upgradation of 500 km of water pipelines
  • Installation of modern water treatment plants
  • Location: Metropolitan Area
  • Duration: 24 months
  • Budget: $30 million allocated
  • Improved water quality by 90%

Flood Control and Drainage System

A critical project aimed at preventing flooding and improving drainage systems, protecting citizens and infrastructure from flood damage.

  • Construction of 10 km of flood control walls
  • Upgradation of 20 km of drainage pipelines
  • Location: Flood-prone areas
  • Duration: 18 months
  • Budget: $20 million allocated
  • Reduced flood risk by 80%
